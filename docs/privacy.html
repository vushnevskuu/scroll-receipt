PRIVACY POLICY

Scroll Receipt · Updated July 10, 2026

PRINCIPLE

Scroll Receipt is local-first. The browser extension measures active short-form viewing on supported websites. The Android app reads only foreground duration for the official Instagram, YouTube, and TikTok apps through Android Usage Access.

ON-DEVICE PROCESSING

  • Browser: active viewing duration, session totals, and engagement counters
  • Android: whole-app foreground duration for Instagram, YouTube, and TikTok
  • Settings such as hourly value, timezone, and receipt preferences
  • A random per-install ID used only to prevent duplicate daily sync records

Android does not expose whether Reels or Shorts is open without more invasive accessibility access. Scroll Receipt does not request an Accessibility Service.

WHAT WE NEVER COLLECT

  • Messages, comments, captions, searches, passwords, or typed text
  • Screenshots, screen recordings, microphone, camera, or precise location
  • Video contents, titles, downloads, or Android screen contents
  • An inventory of installed apps or advertising identifiers
  • Raw browser page URLs; local content identifiers are hashed

STORAGE & SYNC

Local tracking needs no account. If you enable email receipts, sync sends only your verified email, daily aggregate platform totals, local date, timezone, and a random per-install ID. Supabase provides auth, storage, and server functions; Resend delivers requested emails. HTTPS protects data in transit. Data is not sold or used for ads.

YOUR CONTROLS

  • Use local tracking without an account
  • Revoke Android Usage Access in system settings
  • Pause, export, or delete local browser data
  • Disconnect optional email receipts
  • Permanently delete the account and synced data at Delete Account

Server deletion normally completes immediately. Local data can be removed separately from each device. Hosting and email providers may temporarily process standard network and security logs to operate and protect the service.
