## Detailed description

**Know where your short-form time actually goes.**

Scroll Receipt measures confirmed playback on Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, and TikTok in your browser — not background tabs, not idle time.

**Why people install it**
- Real playback tracking (focus + visible + playing)
- Neutral daily receipt — informative, not guilt-driven
- Local-first: your data stays on your device
- Optional daily email summary at 18:00 local time for yesterday's totals
- Pause, export, or delete anytime

**What we track**
- Watch time per platform
- View counts (3s+ confirmed playback)
- Engagement signals: engaged, completed, quick skips

**What we never track**
- Messages, comments, captions, searches
- Full URLs or video titles (hashed IDs only)
- Native mobile apps
- Unrelated browsing

**Scope:** Browser activity only. Receipts are labeled accordingly.

**Install:** Download from GitHub Pages or load unpacked from repo release zip.
