INSTALL (2 MIN)

  1. Download the .zip
  2. Unzip on your computer.
  3. Open chrome://extensions in Chrome, Edge, or Brave.
  4. Turn on Developer mode.
  5. Click Load unpacked → select the unzipped folder.
  6. Pin Scroll Receipt. Watch a Reel or Short to see your first totals.

FAQ

What does it track?
Confirmed playback on Reels, Shorts, and TikTok in your browser — not background tabs or idle time.
Does it work on my phone?
No. Browser only. We say that upfront because accuracy matters.
When does the email receipt arrive?
Default: 00:05 local time, for yesterday. Enable in Options after email verification.
Can I pause or delete my data?
Yes — pause tracking, export JSON, or delete everything from Settings.
