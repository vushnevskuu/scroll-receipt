PRIVACY POLICY

Scroll Receipt · Updated July 2026

PRINCIPLE

Your activity stays on your device by default. Scroll Receipt measures active short-form viewing on supported websites. It does not read messages, comments, captions, searches, passwords, or unrelated browsing activity.

WHAT WE COLLECT

  • Active viewing duration on YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels, TikTok (browser)
  • Engagement metrics: viewed, engaged, completed, quick skips
  • Session timestamps and platform totals
  • User settings: platform toggles, equivalent rates, receipt time

WHAT WE NEVER COLLECT

  • Messages, comments, captions, searches, typed text
  • Screenshots or screen recordings
  • Video downloads
  • Unrelated website activity
  • Raw page URLs (only hashed content identifiers locally)

STORAGE & SYNC

Data is stored in chrome.storage.local on your device. Optional email sync sends only daily aggregate totals (seconds and view counts per platform) to our backend — never video URLs or titles. No analytics SDKs or tracking pixels on the landing page.

YOUR CONTROLS

  • Pause tracking anytime
  • Disable individual platforms
  • Export data as JSON
  • Delete today's data or all data
