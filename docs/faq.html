FAQ

Scroll Receipt

What does it track?
Confirmed playback on Reels, Shorts, and TikTok in your browser — not background tabs or idle time.
Does it work on my phone?
No. Browser only. Native mobile apps are not tracked.
Is my data private?
By default everything stays on your device. Email sync sends only daily totals, never video URLs or titles.
When does the email receipt arrive?
Default: 18:00 local time, for yesterday. Enable it in Options after email verification.
What if the email contains a sign-in link instead of a code?
That works too. Paste the full Supabase sign-in link into the same verification field in Options, and Scroll Receipt will reopen it with the correct callback instead of localhost.
Can I pause or delete my data?
Yes — pause tracking, export JSON, or delete everything from Settings.
